Steps of Candle Making

Creating a soy wax candle may involve the following stages

  1. Materials preparation: small soy wax fragments, candle wick, fragrances, dyes, and other additional materials

  2. Mold preparation: choosing a candle mold and preparing it for wax filling

  3. Wax preparation: melting soy wax in a melting device to the required temperature. Adding fragrances and dyes if necessary

  4. Wick preparation: inserting the wick into the mold and securing it in a way that it's evenly tensioned.

  5. Materials preparation: small soy wax fragments, candle wick, fragrances, dyes, and other additional materials.

  6. Molding: pour the wax into the mold until it fills completely. Wait for it to solidify.
